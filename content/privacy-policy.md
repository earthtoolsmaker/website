*Last updated: June 12, 2026*

EarthToolsMaker is a static website. We do not collect or store personal data ourselves: there are no analytics, no tracking cookies, and no ads. The few places where data is involved at all are described below.

## Hosting

This website is served by [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/). Like any web host, Netlify processes the IP address of your device in standard server logs in order to deliver the site. See [Netlify's privacy policy](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/) for details.

## Contact and sponsor forms

When you submit the form on our [contact](/contact/) or [sponsor](/sponsor/) page, your name, email address, and message are processed by [Formspree](https://formspree.io/) and delivered to us by email. We use this information only to reply to you — never for marketing — and we do not share it with anyone else. See [Formspree's privacy policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/) for details.

## Embedded content and third-party resources

Some pages embed interactive machine learning demos from [Hugging Face Spaces](https://huggingface.co/spaces). The site also loads fonts from Google Fonts and math-rendering assets from the jsDelivr CDN. When a page uses these, your browser contacts those services directly, which exposes your IP address to them. Their handling of that data is governed by their own policies:

- [Hugging Face privacy policy](https://huggingface.co/privacy)
- [Google privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [jsDelivr privacy policy](https://www.jsdelivr.com/terms/privacy-policy-jsdelivr-net)

## Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you — realistically, only the emails you sent us through the forms — and ask us to correct or delete it. Reach out through the [contact page](/contact/) and we will take care of it.

## Questions

If anything in this policy is unclear, please [get in touch](/contact/).
